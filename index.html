    Case study: IoT and Cloud

    A breath of fresh air for pollution management

    Air might be invisible, but it’s not hard to see why monitoring its quality is so important. Despite its significant impact on our health, air quality is surprisingly difficult to assess. Today’s tech is expensive and offers only a cursory assessment that ignores indoor air quality and factors like radiation from electrical devices.

    To provide a simpler way of measuring pollutants, RadGreen developed an integrated environmental monitoring system. Through sensors and the Internet of Things, it can track much more detailed data at scale. And using API Connect and Node-RED, it can rapidly notify its clients about unsafe pollution levels.
